The Engine of MetaCars

MetaCars is a Token + NFT including Play to Earn Metaverse Game inspired by the cartoon Cars. As children, we all watched the cartoon Cars, but now is the time to make our dream come true by creating a community of car enthusiasts. MetaCars is a proprietary, hyper deflationary token on the Ethereum Network. MetaCars is not just a regular token, it is a token where collects all cars cartoon lovers in one community and offers them different kinds of NFTs to use in Metaverse and the cars carton converted into a Metaverse GAME. Our team aims to bring together a community that are car lovers and racing gamers, and this will be by organizing lots of events, organizing CarShows etc.

MetaCars P2E Game

MetaCars P2E Game is the game's utility token that can be used to reward for players participation in tournaments and missions. MetaCarsGame is a 2022 arcade-style racing video game in Metaverse. The game features a racing style that incorporates Cars Cartoon cars and locales with arcade style handling and vehicular combat. This game will be able to play only in Metaverse. Every new player should buy a car with $MetaCarsTokens. Players can upgrade their cars, can buy new cars, also can earn by playing. Another thing is that players that have NFTs, can use those NFTs as cars in the game.
For every game that the players win, they will earn $MCPoints, where they can convert those points into MetaCars tokens. Our team will organize weekly tournament for our game players, where top 10# winners will be rewarded. The reward prize for top 1# player is 500$, where it will increase 10% for every tournament.
